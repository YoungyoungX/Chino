东：

dōng

east

 

东方，东部

dōng fāng ，dōng bù

east, eastern

 

中国在遥远的东方。

Zhōngguó zài yáoyuǎn de dōngfāng

China is in the far east.

 

西：

xī

west

 

西方，西部

Xīfāng，xībù

west, western

我们喜欢看《西部世界》。

Wǒmen xǐhuan kàn “xībù shìjiè”.

We like to watch the Westworld

 

南:

Nán

south

南方，南京

nánfāng, nánjīng

south, Nanjing

 

南京在长江的南面。

nánjīng zài chángjiāng de nánmiàn

Nanjing is on the south side of the Yangtze River

 

北:

běi

north

 

北方，北京

běifāng, běijīng
north, Beijing

 

北京是中国的首都，它在中国的北部。
běijīng shì zhōngguó de shǒudū, tā zài zhōngguó de běibù.

Beijing is the capital of China, which is in the north of China.
